About Us

Serving Excellence Since 2010

Pioneer Software Solutions came into existence with serving the consulting for computerized accounting system since 2010. The major object of the company is to identify itself in the field on computerized accounting system implementation and had chosen Tally to nurture its ideas. It is associated with Tally as a Tally Partner since late December 2013. At present we have 8+ years experience of serving solution on Tally with 10+ team size and more than 200 clients across Nepal.

With our broad technical skill base, our superior project management techniques with long experience of system implementation, we help our client achieve their goals, increase productivity and realize high return on investment. We have a high quality infrastructure with excellent team spirit to ensure maximum reliability and efficiency at all times. Our creative team can understand the client's requirements and deliver tailor-fit solutions. Besides, customized solution is a major wings of our service. Strength of our company lies in its working principle of Customer Satisfaction that leads us to serve the customer database over 200 + within a few span of time.

Who We Are?

At Pioneer, we provide local as well as remote accounting database administration, system implementation, accounting process automation, advisory services, and computerized accounting system audit. Our quality speaks and we are highly customer focused. Our passion is to automate the accounting processes and make things easier. We will capture the potential risks of your financial database and avoid escalations so that you can continue to focus on your business. We are a team of experienced and certified professionals with more than 9 years of experience. We are experienced and certified Tally Professionals. We respond to any incident within 15 minutes. We strive to provide 99.99% uptime of your systems with high compliance.

What We Do?

Primarily we are focused on selling Tally products in Nepal. We gives consulting services on process automation through accounting software with the help of customizing Tally Software or third party applications. Besides that we also provide advisor service on processing MIS reports in different business verticals.
