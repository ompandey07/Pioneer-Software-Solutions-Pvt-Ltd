Biz Analyst

Biz Analyst is a powerful mobile application that syncs securely with your Tally ERP9 or Tally Prime installation, providing real-time access to your business data anytime, anywhere. With multiple reports, dashboards, and graphs, it empowers CEOs, senior management, and team members to make informed decisions in the office, at meetings, or on the road. Available on both iPhone and Android platforms, Biz Analyst delivers critical business insights with just a few clicks. Detailed product features are available in the attached PDF files.
