Customized Statutory Report

There are various business requirements on different business verticals. Default features of Tally Prime gives the standard reports which are common for all business i.e. Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss, Trial Balance, Ledger Report, Bank Books etc. Additional requirements on accounting system as per user’s business need may not be address by default features. For it, we will have to customize it. In Nepal, government enforce certain reports to be filed or uploaded in a regular interval of time by every business organizations and the format of the reports are frequently updated as per their needs. Usually these reports are not available in default features of Tally Prime.
