Tally Prime

It is a globally recognized and most popular over South Asia region and widely used on different business verticals except baking and purely financial institutions. From very beginning from dos mode application, it get recently launched web based services within its existing features and it still moving toward full ERP system within next few years. The updates are available with new features that give us to implement the package more efficiently as per organizational needs. Tally releases two types of versions globally, i.e Tally Prime Silver and Tally Prime Gold.



Tally Prime Silver

Tally Prime Silver can be used for only one computer at a time i.e license is enabled on one machine at a time but it is transferable to another machine whenever required. But the data of local computer can be accessed from remotely via internet through the available service of Tally Software Subscription or we can view it by simply copying the database file to another.



Tally Prime Gold

Tally Prime Gold can be used multiple numbers of computers within the coverage of LAN system. In case of multi-user license, server setup get installed on one server system and its license will be share to another several computers on the LAN. The computer which doesn’t come under LAN coverage can’t able to share the license from the server. But the data at server can also be viewed from remotely via internet through the available service of Tally Software Subscription.

Tally Software Subscription (TSS)

Tally Software Subscription is a technology within Tally Prime which powers revolutionary capabilities such as continuous upgrades and updates, central consolidation of branch data and many more that enhances your business performance. Tally Software Subscription service will be available for one year from the date of first activation i.e it gets expired after completion of one year. But it is not mandatory to subscribe to TSS. Tally Prime will continue to function with all the standard features excepting those that are powered by TSS.

Tally.Server9

Tally.Server9 is an Enterprises Class product that helps fast growth medium and large size business to improve their business effectiveness. It enhances the power and control of Tally Prime Gold users by converting the existing peer-to-peer kind of data access to a server based data management. Tally.Server9 is built with multi-version concurrency capabilities that provide the most efficient; scalability. Requests from every user get equal priority and get solved in parallel with others, rather than sequentially. There is no queuing of access of modification requests and so every user will feel as if he were working with his own local copy or snapshot of data. Tally.Server 9 will ensure that the data access/modification by a user is isolated to his/her own snapshot, thus allowing other users to operate smoothly. With Tally.Server9, there is essentially no blocking of another user’s tasks, even as new users keep getting added to the system, or because of the types of tasks being performed. It also maintains very high data consistency in intense use environment. Overall it leads to lower cost of ownership, lower maintenance and higher efficiency of operations for any medium to large business needing an enterprises grade system
